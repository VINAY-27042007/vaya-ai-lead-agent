# Vaya AI - Company Knowledge

## Company Overview
We help businesses automate their lead generation process using AI-powered voice agents.

## Main Features
- Instant response to ad leads.
- Human-like AI conversations.
- Lead qualification and information collection.
- AI-generated summaries and transcripts.
- Future phone call and CRM integration.

## Mission
Reduce the time between a customer showing interest and receiving a response.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Vaya?
Vaya is an AI voice assistant that can interact with customers, answer questions, and collect lead information automatically.

### Does Vaya replace human agents?
No. Vaya handles initial conversations and basic queries, while human agents can take over for advanced discussions.

### What information does Vaya collect?
Vaya can collect customer name, interest, requirements, preferred contact time, and other lead details.
